🎯 What's the objective?
📬Clients flood your inbox. Each one wants a mortgage. Your job is to figure out who qualifies and who doesn't — then Approve or Deny them before they lose patience and call Rocket Mortgage.
💰You earn commission when approved loans close. High loan amounts = big paychecks. Your score is months survived × total commission earned.
⚡Efficiency matters. Fewer questions asked before a correct decision = more XP. Ask only what you need.

⚠️ How you get fired
🚨Each bad loan approved earns a Compliance Strike. 3 strikes = formal warning + penalty. 5 strikes = you're fired, desk cleared, badge revoked.
🔥Chain correct decisions to build a streak and multiply your XP. Streaks break on bad loans or missed closes.
💡 Pro tips
👀Check the loan amount and mood icon on each bubble before opening. Prioritize whales. Ignore $25k HELOCs from angry people.
